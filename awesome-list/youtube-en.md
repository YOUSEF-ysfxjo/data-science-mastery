# 🎥 YouTube Channels — English

> 7 channels. Subscribe to all of them. Watch based on what you need each week.

---

## ⭐⭐⭐ [3Blue1Brown — Grant Sanderson](https://www.youtube.com/@3blue1brown)
- **Focus:** Mathematics visually
- **Series you must watch:** Essence of Linear Algebra, Essence of Calculus, Neural Networks
- **Why:** No other channel teaches math at this level. For free. Treat it as a requirement.

## ⭐⭐⭐ [StatQuest — Josh Starmer](https://www.youtube.com/@statquest)
- **Focus:** ML + Statistics with intuition
- **Why:** Explains concepts you think are hard in the simplest possible way.
- **Series:** Machine Learning Fundamentals, Neural Networks Clearly Explained

## ⭐⭐⭐ [Andrej Karpathy](https://www.youtube.com/@AndrejKarpathy)
- **Focus:** Deep Learning from scratch
- **Series:** Neural Networks: Zero to Hero
- **Why:** Karpathy is one of the top 3 most important people in AI today. He builds GPT with you.

## ⭐⭐ [Two Minute Papers](https://www.youtube.com/@TwoMinutePapers)
- **Focus:** Visual paper summaries
- **Why:** To stay up-to-date with the latest research, concisely.

## ⭐⭐ [Yannic Kilcher](https://www.youtube.com/@YannicKilcher)
- **Focus:** Deep paper analysis
- **Why:** For those who want to understand a paper line by line.

## ⭐⭐ [DeepMind](https://www.youtube.com/@Google_DeepMind)
- **Focus:** DeepMind research
- **Why:** For context on the latest developments.

## ⭐ [Sentdex](https://www.youtube.com/@sentdex)
- **Focus:** Practical Python tutorials
- **Why:** For a quick start on a new topic.
