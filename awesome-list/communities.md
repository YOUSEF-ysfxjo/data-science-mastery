# 👥 Communities

---

## Arabic

- **[SDAIA Community](https://sdaia.gov.sa/)** — Saudi Data and AI Authority
- **[KAUST Academy Slack/Discord](https://academy.kaust.edu.sa/)** — for enrolled participants
- **WhatsApp/Telegram groups** — almost every Saudi university has an ML research group; ask in your department
- **[Tuwaiq Academy](https://tuwaiq.edu.sa/)** — meetups and training programs

## Global

- **[r/MachineLearning (Reddit)](https://reddit.com/r/MachineLearning)** — the most important subreddit
- **[r/datascience (Reddit)](https://reddit.com/r/datascience)** — more applied focus
- **[Kaggle Discussions](https://www.kaggle.com/discussions)** — under every competition
- **[Hugging Face Discord](https://huggingface.co/discord)** — for NLP
- **[fast.ai Forums](https://forums.fast.ai/)** — active community
- **[ML Twitter/X](https://twitter.com/)** — follow Karpathy, LeCun, Hadfield-Menell, Eugene Yan

## For Publishing

- **LinkedIn** — post your learning. 2 posts/week is enough. The Saudi/Gulf audience is most active here.
- **Twitter/X** — best for global technical discussions.
- **GitHub** — everything goes in your profile.
- **Hashnode/Medium** — for long-form blog posts.
